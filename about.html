ABOUT US





TASTY BITES is a locally and family owned business that was founded in 2006. They are the best food you'll ever experience, with an unforgettable taste. Our food are our passion. Without wonderful customers like you, our RESTAURANT would never survive. That's why we're sure to say "thank you" to everyone who gives us their business and support. We're extremely thankful for our loyal customers and their love never falls short.It's such a unique restaurant that has worn the crown of the best delivering food to its customers.


The specialty of this restaurant is that the food, including all veg and non veg dishes are prepared in earthen vessels and the items are served on banana leaves. The home-ground spices in the food items give a finger-licking taste and for this good taste and healthy dishes people love to visit this restaurant several times. Visiting on Saturdays and Sundays, one should not miss the chance of having the food items of weekend specialties such as Turkey fry and Turkey biryani. Visit TASTY BITES where you'll always be treated like family. Warm smiles and welcome greetings are our number one guarantee. Carryout and delivery options are available. Our food taste as good as they look!
